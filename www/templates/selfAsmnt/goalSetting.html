Goal Setting and Time Management Exercise

Fill in the blanks

List your long-term goals. What would you like to accomplish in the next seven to ten years?: (Examples: Buying a house, getting a M.S or Ph.D., getting married, having a family, moving into management)




List your short-term goals. What would you like to accomplish in the next three to five years? (Example: getting a job, starting a career, getting a car that runs)




List your immediate goals. What would you like to accomplish in the next in the next few days, weeks or months, in the next six months or before the year ends?




On the next page, you are going to set your priorities across all of your goals, listing first those, which are most important.
