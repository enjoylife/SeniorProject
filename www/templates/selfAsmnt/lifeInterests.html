Finding Life Interests and Pursuing Your Passion

Fill in the blanks

This exercise meant to help you focus on interests that you might not ordinarily have thought about as the basis for a career opportunity. The idea is to spend some time thinking about what you really like to spend your time doing, and to think about how your your education, experience and skills might be used in an industry that is aligned with your interests.

Start by making a list of your favorite things and favorite activities. Make your list at least ten items long to make sure you've considered everything that interests you.
