Motivated Skills Inventory Results

The ratings are a combination of motivation plus skill level. These results are important! They can be referenced anytime from your Career Binder.

A skill rating of 8 means you are highly skilled and highly motivated. A score of 7 means you are slightly lacking in motivation or skill, etc.

Skills you rate below a 6 will not be considered.

Focus on your 8s!
